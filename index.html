About Us

We are a former Yamaha School Of Music Philippines Inc. located at the basement of Lopue's San Sebastian providing high-quality music and art lessons for all ages and skill levels...

Voice Lesson

Learn the fundamentals of singing, proper vocal techniques, breathing exercises, and performance skills.

Piano Lesson

Develop your piano skills from beginner to advanced level with music theory and practical exercises.

Violin Lesson

Master the art of violin playing with posture correction, bow techniques, and melody practice.

Lead Guitar Lesson

Learn chords, scales, and solos to become a skilled lead guitarist in any music genre.

Bass Guitar Lesson

Build a strong foundation in rhythm, fingerstyle, and groove to play bass like a pro.

Drums Lesson

Improve your timing, rhythm, and drumming techniques to become an excellent drummer.

Keyboard Lesson

Learn modern keyboard skills, chord progressions, and accompaniment techniques for all styles.

Art Lesson

Explore your creativity with drawing, painting, and other visual arts techniques.
